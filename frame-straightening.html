About This Service
Precision Structural Restoration

A damaged frame is one of the most serious — and most critical — repairs a vehicle can need. Nero's uses a computerized laser measuring system to diagnose and correct frame damage with sub-millimeter precision.

Left unrepaired, a bent frame can affect your vehicle's handling, tire wear, alignment, and structural safety in a crash. Our certified technicians restore your frame to exact OEM specifications.

Every frame repair is verified against manufacturer tolerances before the vehicle moves to the next stage of repair.

How It Works
Our Process
01

Damage Assessment

Full computerized laser measurement to map every deviation from factory specs.

02

Anchoring & Securing

Vehicle is secured to our precision frame bench using factory anchor points.

03

Hydraulic Correction

Controlled hydraulic pressure gradually and precisely moves the frame back to spec.

04

Re-Measurement

After each correction, we re-measure to verify accuracy before continuing.

05

Alignment Check

A full 4-wheel alignment is performed to confirm the chassis geometry is within tolerance.

06

Final Inspection

A certified technician signs off on every measurement before the vehicle proceeds to body repair.

Common Questions
Frame Straightening FAQ
How do I know if my frame is damaged?
Signs include uneven panel gaps, doors that don't close properly, pulling while driving, or uneven tire wear. A collision — even minor — can bend a frame. We'll check it for free.
Is a bent frame totaled?
Not always. Minor to moderate frame damage is often repairable. Severe damage may exceed the vehicle's value. We'll give you an honest assessment.
How accurate is the repair?
Our laser measuring system measures to within 1mm of factory spec. We don't release a vehicle until every measurement is confirmed within tolerance.
Does frame repair affect resale value?
Disclosed frame damage does affect CarFax and resale value. However, a properly repaired and documented frame repair at a certified shop is far better than undisclosed damage.
Will my car be safe to drive after?
Yes. We won't return a vehicle until it meets all structural safety standards. Your safety is our top priority.

Our Story
Three Decades of Craftsmanship

Nero's Auto Body was founded in 1990 with a single bay, a set of hand tools, and an unwavering commitment to doing things the right way. What started as a one-man shop has grown into a full-service auto body facility with a team of I-CAR certified technicians — but the values have never changed.

For over 30 years, we've been a trusted part of this community. We've fixed the cars of parents, and then their kids, and now their grandkids. We treat every vehicle like it belongs to family, because most of the time, it does.

Our motto is simple: do honest work, charge a fair price, and stand behind everything we do. It's worked for 30 years. It's not changing now.

Our Journey
Nero's Through the Years
1990
Nero Marchetti opens a one-bay shop with a hand-painted sign and a promise to do honest work at a fair price.
1998
Shop expands to four bays and adds a dedicated paint booth. First year achieving I-CAR Gold Class certification.
2005
Nero's becomes a Direct Repair Program (DRP) shop for three major insurance carriers. New computerized frame alignment system installed.
2012
Marco Marchetti joins full-time. Shop upgrades to a state-of-the-art spray booth and computerized color mixing system.
2019
Nero's celebrates its 5,000th vehicle restoration. Expands to 8 bays to meet growing demand. Earns Best Auto Body Shop in the county for the second consecutive year.
Today
12 certified technicians, 8+ bays, and the same values that started it all in 1990. Still family owned. Still community focused.

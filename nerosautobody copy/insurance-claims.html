About This Service
We Handle the Hard Part

Dealing with insurance after an accident is stressful. Nero's works directly with all major insurance carriers to handle your claim from start to finish — so you can focus on what matters while we take care of the paperwork.

We are an approved direct repair facility for most major carriers, which means faster approvals, fewer delays, and no hassle for you. We document all damage, submit supplements, and communicate directly with your adjuster.

You always have the right to choose your own repair shop — don't let an insurance company steer you elsewhere. Choose Nero's and let us fight for the quality repair you deserve.

How It Works
Our Process
01

Report Your Claim

Contact your insurance company to open a claim and get a claim number. You do not need to choose a shop yet.

02

Bring Your Vehicle to Nero's

Drop off your vehicle and we'll conduct a thorough damage inspection and document everything.

03

We Contact Your Insurer

We communicate directly with your insurance adjuster, submit our estimate, and handle all documentation.

04

Supplement Negotiation

If additional damage is found during repair, we submit supplements directly to your carrier — no calls needed from you.

05

Repair Begins

Once approved, repairs begin immediately. We keep you updated throughout the process.

06

Final Billing

We bill your insurance directly. You only pay your deductible (if applicable).

Common Questions
Insurance Claims FAQ
Do I have to use the shop my insurance recommends?
No. You have the legal right to choose any repair shop you want. Insurance companies may prefer their DRP shops, but the choice is always yours.
What if my insurance estimate is too low?
This is very common. We submit supplements for any additional damage found during repair. We negotiate directly with your adjuster so you don't have to.
Will my rates go up if I file a claim?
That depends on your policy and who was at fault. If the accident wasn't your fault, your rates typically won't increase. Contact your agent to understand your specific policy.
Do you handle total loss assessments?
We can assist with documentation and damage reporting, but total loss determinations are made by your insurer. We'll give you an honest opinion on whether repair is worthwhile.
How long does the insurance repair process take?
Once approved, most repairs follow normal timelines. Delays usually come from insurer approvals. We push hard for fast approvals so your car is back as soon as possible.

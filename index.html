Why Choose Nero's
01

Seamless Insurance Claim Process

We work with all insurance companies, ensuring that your vehicle is restored to its pre-loss condition. We'll assist with setting you up with a rental, and have repairs completed in a timely manner. Bring us your vehicle and give us your claim number; we take care of the rest.

02

Accurate Repair Process

Our team ensures that every vehicle is treated with care. Every vehicle that is repaired at Nero's, goes through a thorough damage assessment process. This includes a pre and post scan of your vehicles computer system, frame measurement, research on OEM repair procedures specific to the year, make, and model of your vehicle, and more.

03

Lifetime Warranty

We offer a lifetime warranty on all body and paint work. We stand by our work, and strive for perfection on every job. When your vehicle is delivered back to you, we are comfortable guaranteeing the safety, look, and accuracy of repairs.

04

Complimentary Auto Detailing

Every vehicle we repair receives a complete complementary vehicle detail. This includes a complete exterior wash, and full interior detail. We want to return your vehicle to you looking better than when it came in.

About This Service
A Finish That Looks Factory Perfect

Nero's paint and refinishing department uses a state-of-the-art computerized color matching system that reads your vehicle's exact factory paint code — accounting for years of sun exposure, fading, and oxidation — to blend paint into the adjacent panels.

The result is a finish that's indistinguishable from factory paint and backed by our lifetime warranty against peeling, bubbling, or fading.

Common Questions
Paint & Refinishing FAQ
Can you match my exact paint color?
Yes. Our spectrophotometer reads your vehicle's actual paint — including any fading or weathering — and formulates an exact match that blends invisibly with the surrounding panels.
How long does a paint job take?
Single panel repairs typically take 1–3 days. Full vehicle resprays take 5–7 days depending on preparation requirements.
Is the new paint covered by warranty?
Yes. Our paint and refinishing work carries a lifetime warranty against peeling, cracking, bubbling, and fading due to workmanship defects.
Do you do custom paint work?
Yes — we handle everything from subtle pearl and metallic finishes to full custom color changes. Ask us about our custom bodywork service.
What's the difference between a touch-up and a full panel respray?
Touch-ups blend a small area; panel resprays refinish the entire panel for a perfect, seamless result. We'll recommend the right approach for your repair.
